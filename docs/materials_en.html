oct7map, mapping the deaths and kidnapping

A visual representation of the October 7 massacre on a map, with details of the murdered and fallen. The map provides specific information for each victim, including an indication of the accuracy of the location. It also includes links to personal memorial pages, and allows users to upload images or additional text. Open the map: oct7map
